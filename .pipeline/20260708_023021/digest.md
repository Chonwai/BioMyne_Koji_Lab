### BTG Nabs U.S. Marketing Rights to Wellstat's 5-FU Overdose Antidote Candidate
**Priority:** medium | **Tags:** Oncology, Drug Development, Chemotherapy Safety, Licensing Deal
**URL:** https://www.genengnews.com/news/btg-nabs-u-s-marketing-rights-to-wellstats-5-fu-overdose-antidote-candidate

BTG has secured U.S. marketing and distribution rights to Wellstat Therapeutics' uridine triacetate (PN401), an investigational antidote for fluorouracil (5-FU) overdose resulting from dosing errors or impaired drug clearance. Under the deal, BTG will pay Wellstat an up-front fee, milestone payments on NDA acceptance and approval, and royalties on U.S. net sales, while Wellstat retains all non-U.S. rights and remains responsible for completing development, regulatory filings, and manufacturing. Wellstat plans to submit an NDA to the FDA in the first half of 2013 for this indication, with Phase II and Phase III data already showing improved survival, lower toxicity, and a better therapeutic index when uridine triacetate is combined with 5-FU in gastric and pancreatic cancers.
