## Categories within Quantitative Biology

- **q-bio.BM - Biomolecules**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.BM/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.BM/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.BM/current))

DNA, RNA, proteins, lipids, etc.; molecular structures and folding kinetics; molecular interactions; single-molecule manipulation.

- **q-bio.CB - Cell Behavior**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.CB/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.CB/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.CB/current))

Cell-cell signaling and interaction; morphogenesis and development; apoptosis; bacterial conjugation; viral-host interaction; immunology

- **q-bio.GN - Genomics**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.GN/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.GN/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.GN/current))

DNA sequencing and assembly; gene and motif finding; RNA editing and alternative splicing; genomic structure and processes (replication, transcription, methylation, etc); mutational processes.

- **q-bio.MN - Molecular Networks**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.MN/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.MN/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.MN/current))

Gene regulation, signal transduction, proteomics, metabolomics, gene and enzymatic networks

- **q-bio.NC - Neurons and Cognition**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.NC/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.NC/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.NC/current))

Synapse, cortex, neuronal dynamics, neural network, sensorimotor control, behavior, attention

- **q-bio.OT - Other Quantitative Biology**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.OT/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.OT/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.OT/current))

Work in quantitative biology that does not fit into the other q-bio classifications

- **q-bio.PE - Populations and Evolution**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.PE/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.PE/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.PE/current))

Population dynamics, spatio-temporal and epidemiological models, dynamic speciation, co-evolution, biodiversity, foodwebs, aging; molecular evolution and phylogeny; directed evolution; origin of life

- **q-bio.QM - Quantitative Methods**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.QM/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.QM/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.QM/current))

All experimental, numerical, statistical and mathematical contributions of value to biology

- **q-bio.SC - Subcellular Processes**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.SC/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.SC/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.SC/current))

Assembly and control of subcellular structures (channels, organelles, cytoskeletons, capsules, etc.); molecular motors, transport, subcellular localization; mitosis and meiosis

- **q-bio.TO - Tissues and Organs**
( [new](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.TO/new),
[recent](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.TO/recent),
[current month](https://arxiv.org/list/q-bio.TO/current))

Blood flow in vessels, biomechanics of bones, electrical waves, endocrine system, tumor growth
