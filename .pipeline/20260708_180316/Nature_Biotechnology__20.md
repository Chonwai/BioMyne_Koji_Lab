The [Original Article](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41587-026-03054-y) was published on 18 March 2026

Correction to: _Nature Biotechnology_ [https://doi.org/10.1038/s41587-026-03054-y](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41587-026-03054-y), published online 18 March 2026.

In the version of this article initially published, there was an error in the Fig. 4n Source Data, where an additional “anti-EGFR” dataset was accidentally included; an error in the Fig. 6d Source Data, with errors in the PBS group measurements, also affecting the plots in the article; errors in the Figs. 4o, 5b–d, h Source Data, due to data entry errors; and errors in the Fig. 1a, b Source Data, where decimal-place formatting was inconsistent. The Source Data and Fig. 6d have been revised in the HTML and PDF versions of the article.
