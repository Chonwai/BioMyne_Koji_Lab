**Vertex Pharmaceuticals will spend $10 billion to acquire Crinetics Pharmaceuticals and its drug for a rare endocrine disorder**, [STAT](https://www.statnews.com/2026/07/06/vertex-acquires-crinetics-pharmaceuticals/) reports. Vertex will pick up the Crinetics drug Palsonify, which was launched last year and treats a rare endocrine disorder called acromegaly, as well as other drug candidates that have blockbuster potential if approved. The company is also in the late stages of developing a therapy for congenital adrenal hyperplasia. The deal is the largest acquisition in the history of Vertex, as the Boston-based drugmaker has been on a mission to build out the next generation of products beyond its cystic fibrosis portfolio.
