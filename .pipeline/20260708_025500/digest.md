### Antag, a startup backed by Versant and Novo, joins hunt for obesity drug alternatives
**Priority:** high | **Tags:** obesity, drug-development, GIP-antagonist
**URL:** https://www.biopharmadive.com/news/antag-versant-novo-gip-antagonist-obesity/734505

Danish startup Antag Therapeutics has raised $84 million in a Series A round led by Versant Ventures to advance AT-7687, an experimental GIP antagonist for weight loss. Unlike GLP-1 agonists that activate the GIP receptor, AT-7687 blocks it — a different approach still under scientific debate — with the backing of cofounders University of Copenhagen professors Jens Holst and Mette Rosenkilde.

### Gilead Axes Phase III Trial in Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis
**Priority:** medium | **Tags:** respiratory diseases, Phase III clinical trials, drug development, translational medicine
**URL:** https://www.genengnews.com/news/gilead-axes-phase-iii-trial-in-idiopathic-pulmonary-fibrosis

Gilead Sciences is halting its Phase III trial of ambrisentan (Letairis) in patients with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF) after an interim Data Monitoring Committee analysis found no evidence of treatment benefit. The decision reinforces what analysts describe as IPF being a 'graveyard' for clinical trials, following similar failures by Actelion's Tracleer and highlighting the high barriers to entry for drug development in this disease area.
